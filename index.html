THE KID

Keep him safe

Synopsis

A woman and young boy are chased through the streets of downtown Los Angeles. Battered and exhausted, they protect something highly valuable and extremely dangerous.

About

THE KID is a 6 minute proof-of-concept short film directed by Nicholas Wenger, starring Ellen Wroe (For All Mankind, Animal Kingdom) and Evan Alex (Us). The film has been screened at festivals worldwide, on DirecTV's ShortsHD network, and on DUST (Gunpowder & Sky) where it has garnered 60M+ views online.

The feature film, TRANSFERENCE, is currently in development.
